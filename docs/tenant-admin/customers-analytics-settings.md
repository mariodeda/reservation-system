# Tenant Customers, Analytics, And Settings

## Clients & Statistics

The tenant header groups Customers and Analytics under Clients & Statistics.
Desktop uses a dropdown. Mobile uses a compact selector.

## Customers

The customers page supports searching guest records by name, email, and phone.
Customer detail shows reservation history and upcoming visits.

## Analytics

Analytics summarize restaurant performance over the selected period, including:

- Reservations.
- Covers.
- Guests.
- Service breakdowns.
- Lead-time and no-show indicators.
- Customer trends.

## Settings

Tenant settings are for restaurant-local preferences and staff password changes.
Platform-only controls such as SMTP, public key, allowed origins, and email flow
policy are not exposed to tenant staff.

Password changes are disabled during platform impersonation.
